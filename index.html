About the Institute of Digital Risk

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training institute dedicated to developing the next generation of digital risk practitioners. We combine rigorous academic collaboration with real-world industry projects to address the critical challenges facing modern organizations in the realms of cyber risk, digital resilience, and AI governance.

Through partnerships with leading UK universities and close collaboration with regulated financial services and critical infrastructure organizations, IDR delivers cutting-edge training programs, research initiatives, and advisory services. We empower professionals to implement and maintain frameworks like NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2, ensuring organizations can operate securely and resiliently in an increasingly digital world.
